Burnham Park

Burnham Park, dubbed the city's "Mother of all Parks," is one of Baguio's most popular tourist destinations. It was named after American architect and urban planner Daniel Burnham, who created the park's original design and arrangement. Burnham Park is an open green park at the heart of Baguio.
